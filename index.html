Testing a livestream, but the WIFI sucks?

Don't worry. We got you.

Below is a way to simulate a livestream to your application without having to run OBS or some other broadcasting software yourself.

Just enter your Stream Key below to trigger a 10-minute livestream to your RTMP ingest endpoint. You can see the stream in the Studio Dashboard or through the Livepeer Player in your app.
